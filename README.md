1.# Pizza_Sales_Dashboard
🍕 Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard
A dynamic and interactive data visualization solution designed to analyze pizza sales performance—focusing on revenue trends, customer demand, product popularity, and business growth insights.

📌 2. Short Description / Purpose
The Pizza Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report that helps analyze sales performance across different pizza categories, sizes, and time periods. It enables businesses to identify top-performing products, optimize pricing strategies, and understand customer ordering behavior for better decision-making.

⚙️ 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for creating interactive dashboards and reports
• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, shaping, and transformation
• 🧠 DAX – Used for calculated measures like total revenue, AOV, and KPIs
• 📁 File Formats – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews

📊 4. Data Source
Source: Pizza Sales Dataset (Transactional Data)
The dataset contains detailed records of pizza orders, including:
Order ID, Date, and Time
Pizza Name, Category (Veg/Non-Veg), and Size
Quantity and Unit Price
Total Order Value

🚀 5. Features
🔴 Business Problem
In the food and restaurant industry, especially in pizza businesses, large volumes of transactional data are generated daily. However, businesses struggle to extract meaningful insights such as:

Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
What are the peak sales hours and days?
Which sizes and categories are most preferred?
How to increase average order value?

Without a structured dashboard, decision-making becomes slow and inefficient.

🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive and visually engaging dashboard that:
Tracks overall sales performance in real time
Identifies top-selling and low-performing pizzas
Analyzes customer ordering patterns
Helps improve pricing, marketing, and inventory decisions
📈 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
🔹 Key KPIs 
💰 Total Revenue
📦 Total Orders
🍕 Total Pizzas Sold
💵 Average Order Value

👉 These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

🔹 Sales Trend Over Time (Line / Area Chart)
Displays revenue trends across days/months
Helps identify peak sales periods and seasonal demand
🔹 Top 5 & Bottom 5 Pizzas (Bar Charts)
Highlights best-selling and worst-performing pizzas
Useful for menu optimization and promotions
🔹 Sales by Category (Donut chart)
Shows contribution of categories like Veg, Non-Veg, Classic, etc.
Helps understand customer preferences
🔹 Sales by Size (Bar Chart)
Compares performance of sizes (Small, Medium, Large, XL)
Helps in pricing and inventory planning
🔹 Orders by Day(Bar Chart)
Identifies peak and busiest days
Useful for staffing and operational planning
🔹 Monthly/Weekly Trends (Area Chart)
Tracks performance growth over time
Helps in forecasting demand

💡 Business Impact & Insights
• Menu Optimization:
Identify top-selling pizzas and remove or improve low-performing items
• Revenue Growth:
Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing categories
• Customer Insights:
Understand ordering patterns (weekends, evenings, etc.)
• Operational Efficiency:
Optimize staffing and inventory based on demand trends
• Strategic Decisions:
Improve pricing strategies and introduce targeted offers
